About

I’ve worked for a long time with a close-knit group of guys in a small town just north of Chattanooga, TN. But over the years we’ve all found our own ways to new and exciting opportunities all over the country and now I’m searching for mine. I, along with my young family, am excited to find a new city and company with a team of people who are passionate about their work and take pride in their product. As a family man, I look forward to making a difference, even if only small, in order to make a better world for my daughter. I feel a great way I can do that is to be an example of a diligent worker who looks forward to every day because I get to do what I love most with people I’m proud to work with.
